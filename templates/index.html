mypastes

MyPastes lets you share text quickly.

Simply go to http://mypast.es/EXAMPLE (or http://mypast.es/cats or http://mypast.es/hacking) and get pastey!

No more emailing yourself links or logging into chat just to send someone a unix command.

Works great on desktop and mobile.
